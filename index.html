Our Products

Premium hay varieties for your pet store customers

Timothy Hay

The gold standard for small animal nutrition.

Our premium timothy hay is carefully harvested and stored to maintain maximum freshness and nutritional value. It's the perfect staple food for rabbits, guinea pigs, chinchillas, and other small herbivores.

Key Benefits:

  • High fiber content for digestive health
  • Low protein and calcium (ideal for adult animals)
  • Supports dental health through natural chewing
  • Dust-free and allergen-friendly

Available Sizes:

Small Bales (5-10 lbs) Medium Bales (20-30 lbs) Large Bales (50+ lbs) Compressed Packages

Alfalfa Hay

Nutrient-dense hay for growing and nursing animals.

Our premium alfalfa hay provides exceptional nutritional value with higher protein and calcium content. Perfect for young, pregnant, or nursing animals, as well as underweight pets needing additional nutrition.

Key Benefits:

  • High protein for growth and development
  • Rich in calcium for strong bones
  • Excellent palatability - animals love it!
  • Energy-dense for active animals

Available Sizes:

Small Bales (5-10 lbs) Medium Bales (20-30 lbs) Large Bales (50+ lbs) Compressed Packages

Who We Are

A-OK Hay is a dedicated supplier of premium timothy and alfalfa hay, specializing in wholesale distribution to pet stores across Alberta.

We understand that pet stores need reliable, high-quality hay products to meet the needs of their customers' beloved small animals. Our mission is to provide consistent, fresh, and nutritious hay that pet store owners can trust and recommend with confidence.

Based in Alberta, we source our hay locally, ensuring that every bale meets our strict quality standards. We work directly with experienced hay farmers who share our commitment to producing the finest feed for small herbivores.

Why Choose A-OK Hay?

Locally Sourced Quality

All our hay is sourced from trusted Alberta farms. We personally inspect every harvest to ensure it meets our high standards for color, texture, and nutritional content.

Reliable Delivery

We understand that running out of hay isn't an option for pet stores. Our delivery schedule is consistent and dependable, ensuring your shelves are always stocked.

Competitive Wholesale Pricing

We offer competitive wholesale rates with volume discounts. Our goal is to help your business succeed by providing excellent products at fair prices.

Quality Guarantee

Every bale is carefully inspected and stored in climate-controlled facilities. We guarantee freshness, minimal dust, and optimal nutritional value in every shipment.
